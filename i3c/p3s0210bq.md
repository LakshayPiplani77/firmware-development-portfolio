## Device Overview

The P3S0210BQ is a bidirectional I3C switch designed to enable flexible I3C bus connectivity and device routing.

**Interface:** I3C  
**Category:** I3C Switch

---

## Project Summary

Developed firmware support and demonstration applications for validating the P3S0210BQ I3C switch on NXP hardware platforms.

The project demonstrates switch control, device connectivity management, and I3C bus operation.
