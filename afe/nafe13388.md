## Device Overview

The NAFE13388 is a high-performance Analog Front-End (AFE) device from NXP designed for industrial sensing and measurement applications.

**Interface:** SPI  
**Category:** Analog Front-End (AFE)

## Project Summary

Contributed to application examples and validation workflows for the NAFE13388 device using NXP MCUXpresso SDK.

The project focuses on device configuration, channel setup, data acquisition, and industrial sensing demonstrations.
