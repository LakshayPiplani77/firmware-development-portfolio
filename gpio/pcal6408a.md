## Device Overview

The PCAL6408A is an 8-bit I2C GPIO expander from NXP that extends available GPIO functionality through a serial interface.

**Interface:** I2C  
**Category:** GPIO Expander

---

## Project Summary

Developed a CMSIS-based bare-metal driver and demonstration application for the PCAL6408A GPIO expander.

The project demonstrates GPIO configuration, input/output management, and hardware validation.
